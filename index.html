What is PocketTask?

PocketTask is a simple todo list that avoids complex functionality and instead focuses on basic core features with simple usage. The app is suitable for anyone who needs to quickly write down items such as daily tasks and shopping lists, or it can be used to capture ideas and create simple lists ⭐️

✨ Features ✨

  • folder Create groups for your tasks.
  • swipe Change task groups by swipe.
  • push_pin Pin your tasks to prioritize them.
  • view_list See all tasks on one place.
  • palette Change color theme as your preference.
  • import_export Export/Import your tasks not to lose your data.
  • tune Tweak the design as your choice.
