Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Security

PocketTask is designed with privacy in mind. All your task data is stored securely on your device only. We do not store any of your personal task information in the cloud or on our servers.

In-App Purchases

PocketTask offers premium features through in-app purchases and subscriptions. We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to process and manage these transactions securely.

Data Collected for Purchases

  • Purchase receipts and transaction IDs
  • Subscription status and renewal dates
  • Device identifiers for purchase verification
  • App usage data related to premium features

How We Use This Data

  • Verify legitimate purchases and prevent fraud
  • Manage subscription renewals and cancellations
  • Provide access to premium features
  • Generate anonymized analytics about feature usage

RevenueCat Privacy

RevenueCat processes purchase data on our behalf and follows strict data protection standards. They may collect additional technical data for fraud prevention and service optimization. For more details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

  • Access your purchase history through your device's app store
  • Cancel subscriptions anytime through your app store settings
  • Request deletion of your account data (purchase records may be retained for legal compliance)

Data Retention

Purchase data is retained as long as necessary for subscription management and legal obligations (typically 7 years for tax purposes).

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support section.
